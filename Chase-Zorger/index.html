One Of My Favorite Video Games

Dead By Daylight

Dead By Daylightis a multiplayer horror game where you can choose between playing a survior or a killer. The survior's goal is to repair 5 generator scattered around the map, and once the generators have been repaired the exit gate can be opened in order for the surviors to escape. The killer's goal is to stop the surviors from repairing the generators by downing the survior and putting them on hooks. Once a survior has been hooked 3 times they have offical lost game, and it is up to their other teamates to escape fromm the killer. The gameplay loop revolves around a cat and mouse play style where the killer chases the surviors until the surviors escape, or the killer catches all the surviors.
