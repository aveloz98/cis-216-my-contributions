Life as a College athlete

\

Being a student athlete comes with its challenenges

Balancing the demands of being a college athlete with working and maintaining a home life can be incredibly challenging.Athletes already have
a packed schedule with practices, workouts, games, and travel. Adding a job and family responsibilities on top of that can leave little time
for rest or relaxation. Juggling schoolwork, work shifts, and home life requires efficient time management. Missing one commitment can have
a domino effect on others.

Tips & tricks for managing the schedule

  1. Create a list of tasks
  2. Use each activity as a break from the other (practice is a break from school, work a break from practice ect.)
  3. Get plenty of sleep

Cordinate with your peers

Cooperate with your teammates, classmates, and coworkers to help you succeed. Peer cooperation provides a chance for mutual encouragement.
When everyone is pushing toward the same goal, it's easier to stay focused and energized. Collaborating with peers exposes you to
different approaches and ideas. Maybe a peer has a more efficient way of tackling a task, and by working together, you can learn new
